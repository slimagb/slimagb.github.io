Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my work and collaboration

What is your primary focus area in data science?

My primary focus is integrating statistical rigor with machine learning innovation for healthcare and development data. This includes:

  • Biostatistics and epidemiological analysis
  • Causal inference and program evaluation
  • Agricultural analytics and climate-sensitive applications
  • Predictive modeling for public health surveillance
  • Advanced machine learning for complex data problems

I'm particularly passionate about applying data science methods to development challenges in Africa.

What programming languages do I work with?

I have advanced proficiency in multiple programming languages:

  • R (Advanced) - Primary tool for statistical analysis and visualization
  • Python - For machine learning, data engineering, and web development
  • SQL - Database management and complex queries
  • Stata - Econometric analysis and specialized statistics
  • SPSS - User-friendly statistical analysis
  • Git - Version control and collaboration

I'm proficient in both traditional statistical programming and modern data science frameworks.

Do you offer consulting services?

Yes! I offer consulting services through my freelance work with BDA Institute and on a project basis. I can help with:

  • Data science strategy and architecture design
  • Statistical analysis and methodology selection
  • Machine learning model development and deployment
  • Data engineering pipeline design
  • Business intelligence dashboard development
  • Training and capacity building

For consulting inquiries, please contact me at slim.agbahoungba@ept.edu.sn with details about your project.

What is your research on agricultural yield prediction?

My primary research integrates classical statistical models with deep learning approaches to predict agricultural yields in climate-sensitive contexts. The work addresses:

  • Prediction accuracy improvement through ensemble methods
  • Climate variability and uncertainty quantification
  • Application to smallholder farmers in resource-limited settings
  • Spatio-temporal modeling of crop performance
  • Integration with meteorological and soil data

This manuscript is currently in preparation for submission to Springer Nature.

Are you interested in research collaboration?

Absolutely! I'm actively seeking research collaborations in several areas:

  • Epidemiological surveillance systems with AI integration
  • Agricultural productivity under climate change
  • Health economics and program evaluation
  • Educational data analysis and policy research
  • Development impact assessment methodologies

If you have interesting research questions or datasets, I'd love to discuss potential collaborations. Reach out through my contact page!

How do you approach complex data science problems?

My approach combines rigorous methodology with practical implementation:

  • Problem Definition - Understanding business/research objectives and constraints
  • Data Exploration - Comprehensive EDA and quality assessment
  • Methodology Selection - Choosing appropriate statistical/ML methods based on context
  • Model Development - Building, validating, and comparing multiple approaches
  • Interpretation - Translating technical results into actionable insights
  • Implementation - Deploying solutions with documentation and training

I prioritize interpretability, reproducibility, and practical impact.

  • Google BigQuery - Analytics and machine learning pipelines
  • Databricks - Apache Spark and ML workflows
  • Azure Synapse - Enterprise data warehousing

How do you approach statistical validity and reproducibility?

Reproducibility and transparency are core to my work:

  • Version control (Git/GitHub) for all code and documentation
  • Clear methodology documentation and assumption testing
  • Sensitivity analyses and robustness checks
  • Open-source publishing of research code and data
  • Adherence to statistical best practices and standards
  • Peer review and feedback incorporation

All research code is available on my GitHub for transparency and collaboration.

What's your experience with business intelligence and dashboards?

  • Power BI - Interactive dashboards with DAX formulas
  • Tableau - Advanced visualization and storytelling
  • R Shiny - Custom interactive web applications
  • Qlik - In-memory analytics and exploration

I specialize in translating complex data into compelling visual narratives that drive decision-making.

  • Research Collaboration - Joint research projects on mutual interests
  • Training & Capacity Building - Workshops and training programs on data science topics
  • Advisory - Strategic guidance on data science initiatives
  • Contract Work - Development of specific analyses or tools

What's your approach to ethical data science?

Ethical considerations are central to all my work:

  • Transparent communication about model limitations and uncertainties
  • Privacy protection and responsible data handling
  • Bias detection and mitigation in algorithms and analyses
  • Fair representation in data and results
  • Environmental consideration in resource-intensive computations
  • Focus on benefiting communities and individuals affected by applications

I'm committed to using data science to create positive impact while respecting ethical boundaries.

How do you stay current with data science trends?

  • Regular professional certifications and training (Google Analytics IQ, FAO, UNECA modules)
  • Active engagement with research communities (LTISI Lab at EPT)
  • Publication in peer-reviewed venues
  • Participation in conferences and workshops
  • Following academic literature and technical blogs
  • Experimentation with new tools and methodologies

I'm committed to staying at the forefront of data science innovation and best practices.
